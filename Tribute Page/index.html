Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

Former President Dr.A.P.J.Abdul Kalam with Pranab Mukharjee

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist who served as the 11th president of india from 2002 to 2007. He is known as the Missile Man of India for his work on the dvelopment of Ballistic MIssile and Launch Vehicle Technology. He was keen on encouraging the students in india to take active interest in the emerging technologies.

  • 1931-Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
  • 1954- Graduated in Physics from University of Madras.
  • 1960- Graduated inAerospace Engneering from Madras Institute of technology.
  • 1961-Joined DRDO as a Scientist
  • 1969- Joined Space Research at ISRO.
  • 1980- Became the project Director for India's First Indegenous Satellite Program.
  • 1981-Awarded Padma Bhushan.
  • 1990-Awarded Padam Vibhushan.
  • 1992-Chief advisor for the India's Nuclear Program.
  • 1997-Awarded Bharat Ratan.
  • 2002- Became 11th President of India.
  • 2015-The people's President passes away while doing what he loved the most, addressing students.
